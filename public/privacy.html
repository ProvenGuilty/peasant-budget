Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 7, 2025

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or have access to your financial data. Your data stays on your device or in your own cloud storage account (Google Drive, etc.). We believe in "free as in freedom."

1. Introduction

Peasant Budget ("we," "our," or "the app") is a personal budgeting application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our service.

Our core philosophy is "free as in freedom" — you own your data, and we designed the app so we never have access to it.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, process, or have access to:

  • Your financial transactions or budget data
  • Your bank account information
  • Your income or expense details
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Your location data
  • Your contacts or calendar

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

You choose where your data is stored:

  • Local Storage: Data is stored only in your browser on your device. It never leaves your device.
  • Google Drive: Data is stored in YOUR Google Drive account, in a hidden app-specific folder. We cannot access your Google Drive or see your data.
  • Other Cloud Providers: Similar to Google Drive — data goes directly to YOUR account with that provider.

4. Google Drive Integration

If you choose to use Google Drive storage:

  • We use the drive.appdata scope, which only allows access to a hidden, app-specific folder
  • We cannot see, access, or read any other files in your Google Drive
  • Authentication tokens are stored in your browser's session storage and cleared when you close the browser
  • You can revoke access at any time via your Google Account settings

5. Encryption

For local storage, you may optionally enable encryption:

  • Your data is encrypted using AES-256-GCM
  • Encryption uses a passphrase that only you know
  • We do not store or have access to your passphrase
  • If you forget your passphrase, your data cannot be recovered

6. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or any analytics service
  • Tracking pixels or cookies for advertising
  • Any third-party tracking scripts

The app may use essential cookies for functionality (e.g., remembering your storage provider preference), but these do not track you.

7. Third-Party Services

The app may integrate with third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Drive: Google Privacy Policy
  • Vercel (Hosting): Vercel Privacy Policy

8. Data Export and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: You can export all your data as a JSON file at any time
  • Delete: You can delete all your data from within the app
  • For local storage: Clear your browser data or use the app's delete function
  • For Google Drive: Delete the app data folder or revoke app access

9. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
